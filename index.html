About me

Jiaming Qi received the B.S. degree from the Northeast Agricultural University, in 2016, and the M.S. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology, in 2018. I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D candidate majoring in Control Science and Engineering within Harbin Institute of Technology under the supervision from Prof. Guangfu Ma. My main research interests include visual servoing, shape servoing, deformable objects manipulation, human-robot interaction, deep learning, and adaptive control. Also, I do some research about Computer Vision, such as shape estimation and reconstruction of soft objects, and shape-data compression. I focus on using control theory combined with neural network to solve practical problems in the field of robotics.
